Sri Chaitanya Techno School is a minor part of an ongoing education scam in Andhra Pradesh by the Sri Chaitanya Educational Group [1]. Since its inception in the mid-80s, the group has been promoting rote learning under the pretext of revolutionizing education in the then state of Andhra Pradesh.

Sri Chaitanya started in 1986 with a girls' junior college in Vijayawada. [3] Sri Chaitanya Techno School (also called SCTS) was opened in Eluru in June 2010 on the Santhinagar Main road.
